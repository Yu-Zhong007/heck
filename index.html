HOW TO BUY?

Step 1: Create a wallet

We recommend to download the Phantom wallet from the app store or google play. If you are a desktop user then download the Google Chrome extension through the official phantom website. You can also try other Solana compatible wallets

Step 2: Get SOL balance

To be able to buy you will need SOL in your Phantom wallet to swap it for $HECK tokens. You can buy SOL directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy it on an exchange and send it to your DeFi wallet.

Step 3: Go to Raydium

Open Raydium. Connect the wallet that you funded. Make sure to paste the official $HECK token address that is stated on the website, and confirm. Carefully follow all further instructions inside your wallet.

Step 4: Swap for $HECK

Swap your SOL for $HECK. After you follow further instructions in your phantom wallet and have confirmed the transaction then you are a new holder of $HECK! Lets go to the moon together <3
